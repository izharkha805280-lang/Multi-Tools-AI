Understanding Your BMI (Body Mass Index)

BMI is a simple calculation using a person's height and weight. The result is an indicator of whether you have a healthy body weight for your height. Our Online BMI Calculator helps you track your health journey instantly.

What do the Results Mean?

üîπ Underweight (BMI < 18.5): You might need to gain weight for better health.

üîπ Normal Weight (18.5 - 24.9): Great job! You are in the healthy zone.

üîπ Overweight (25 - 29.9): It might be time to adopt healthier eating habits.

Why Track BMI?

Keeping an eye on your BMI can prevent health risks like heart disease, diabetes, and high blood pressure. Use this tool weekly to monitor your progress.
